Log Files

Limadetik follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services’ analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movement on the website, and gathering demographic information. Our Privacy Policy was created with the help of the Privacy Policy PensNews.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device by a website to retain user information and settings. They are widely used for purposes like remembering login credentials, keeping items in shopping carts, and tracking browsing behavior to deliver personalized content or advertisements. Web beacons, also known as tracking pixels or clear GIFs, are tiny, often invisible image files embedded in a webpage or email. They gather information about the user's actions, such as opening an email or visiting a particular section of a website. Web beacons are commonly used in combination with cookies to analyze user interactions and improve the website's effectiveness.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

The DoubleClick DART cookie tracks users across multiple websites by storing information about the pages visited, links clicked, and other relevant activity. This cookie is used to create a profile of user interests, allowing Google’s advertising network to serve more relevant ads to each individual. For instance, if a user frequently visits websites about travel, the DoubleClick cookie may ensure they receive ads related to travel deals or destinations.
